Timeline Of Swami Vivekanand's Life

1863 : (12 October) Birth in Kolkata

1881 : First meeting with Sri Ramakrishna

1884 : Passes B. A. Examination

1887 : Formal vows of sannyasa at Baranagar Monastery

1893 : First public lecture, Secunderabad, South India

1894 : Begins lectures and classes on East Coast

1895 : Begins classes in New York

1896 : Speaks at Harvard University, offered Eastern Philosophy chair

1897 : Arrives in Colombo, Sri Lanka

1899 : Returns to Kolkata

1900 : Arrives in San Francisco

1901 : Pilgrimage in East Bengal and Assam

1902 : Mahasamadhi (4 July)
